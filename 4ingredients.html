Breakfast: 4 Ingredient Scrambled Tofu

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms, chopped
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • 1/2 block medium-firm tofu
  • salt to taste
  • salsa

    • Preparation

    Step 1: Dice mushrooms and bell peppers and fry over medium heat in a non-stick pan with a bit of oil and sprinkle with salt. Cook until mushrooms are browned.
    Step 2: Add medium-firm tofu (crumble it with your fingers) and mix with the vegetables. Let it sit for a couple of minutes until the tofu browns then mix well with the vegetables.
    Step 3: Add as much salsa as wanted for flavour and salt to taste. Enjoy!

Lunch: 4 Ingredient Tempeh Collard Green Wraps

Ingredients

  • Collard greens
  • Green bell pepper
  • Brown rice
  • Oil and salt

    • Preparation

    Step 1: Trim the ends of the stems off the collard greens and cut the green pepper into long strips.
    Step 2: Heat some oil in a non-stick pan and add the tempeh and green pepper. Fry over medium heat. Flip the tempeh once and remove once it has browned; once green peppers have cooked remove them from the pan as well.
    Step 3: Bring a pot of water to a soft boil and blanch the collard green leaves by placing them in the hot water for 30 seconds each, then put them in ice cold water to stop the cooking process.
    Step 4: Lay a single collard green leaf flat and spread some brown rice in the middle. Place a couple of pieces of temphe and some bell peppers horizontally and wrap the leaf up as you would do a burrito
    Step 5: Cut in half and serve. Enjoy!

Dinner: Lasagne Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, chopped
  • 1/2 cup red split lentils (soak in water for about 15 minutes to quicken cooking process)
  • 1 cup pasta sauce
  • 3-4 lasagne sheets
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh basil, chopped
  • Oil

    • Preparation

    Step 1: Chop mushrooms and heat up a pot with some oil.
    Step 2: Cook mushrooms for afew minutes, then add lentils, water and pasta sauce. Mix well and bring to a boil.
    Step 3: Break apart lasagne sheets and add to the mixture.
    Step 4: Let it simmer it simmer for around 15-20 minutes and you are done.
    Step 5: Add some salt to taste and some chopped basil for garnish. Enjoy!
